# Research Prompt: Sonique Audio Player Interface Design

**Date:** December 18, 2025  
**For:** Gemini (or external researcher)  
**Context:** Designing Cortana Personal AI interface - need Sonique inspiration  
**Purpose:** Understand what made Sonique interfaces so innovative and cool

---

## Background

We're designing an interface for a personal AI assistant (Cortana, Halo-inspired). The user remembers **Sonique** - an early 2000s audio player known for experimental, curved, non-rectangular interfaces that were WAY cooler than standard Winamp skins.

We want to understand:
- What made Sonique special?
- How were the interfaces designed?
- What technical tricks did they use?
- What can we learn/steal for a modern interface?

---
